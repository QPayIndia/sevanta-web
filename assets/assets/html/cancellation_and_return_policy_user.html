CANCELLATION, RETURNS & REFUND POLICY

Sevanta is committed to offering a transparent and reliable shopping and service experience. This policy applies to all purchases made on Sevanta, including both products and services.

Important: Seller / Service provider may have their own cancellation, return, and refund policies, which will be clearly displayed on the product/service/seller page. In such cases, the seller's policy will apply in addition to Sevanta's base policy.

1. Cancellation Policy

Products

  • Before Seller Confirmation: Full refund with no charges.
  • After Seller Confirmation but Before Shipment: Cancellation allowed with a Rs. 20 cancellation fee.
  • Once Shipped:
    • Cancellation not allowed. Customers may opt for Returns after delivery (check the Returns Policy).
    • If refused at the doorstep → treated as Return-to-Origin (RTO); refund issued after Seller receives the product and after appropriate shipping fee deductions.
    • Repeated refusals may lead to shipping fee deductions or account restrictions.
  • If the Seller Cancels the Order: Full refund to the customer, no charges.

Services

  • Cancellation >24 hours before service time: Full refund.
  • Cancellation between 24–2 hours before service: 30% cancellation fee.
  • Cancellation <2 hours before service time: No refund.
  • If the Service Provider Cancels: Full refund to the customer.

2. Returns & Refunds Policy

Products

  • Return Window: Most products are returnable within 7–10 days of delivery (exact window shown on product page of the seller).
  • Eligible Returns: Damaged, defective, missing parts, or item different from description.
  • Return Conditions:
    • Product must be unused, in original condition, with brand/manufacturer box, tags, manuals, and accessories intact.
    • Electronics must be cleared of all personal information before return. Sevanta is not liable for misuse of residual data.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Perishables, personal/hygiene items, custom-made orders, or items marked “Non-Returnable.”
  • Refund/Replacement:
    • If replacement stock is available with the same seller → replacement will be offered.
    • If unavailable → refund will be issued.
  • Refund Method: Credited to the original payment instrument.
  • Refund Timeline: 7 to 15 business days after receipt of the return item by the seller and quality check.

Services

  • If service quality issues are reported within 24 hours of completion, Service Provider may provide:
    • A partial refund, or
    • A free re-service (depending on the nature of the complaint and investigation).
  • Please check the Refund Policy on the Service Provider Page.

3. General Policy

  • Products or services marked with “Non-Returnable/Non-Cancellable” on the listing page will follow those rules.
  • In the event of seller/service provider cancellations, customers will always receive a full refund.
  • Users found to misuse the cancellation or return policy by excessive cancellations, refusals, or false claims may face account warnings, suspension, or termination.

NOTE: Sevanta reserves the right to accept the cancellation of any order. Sevanta also reserves the right to waive off or modify the time window or cancellation fee from time to time.
